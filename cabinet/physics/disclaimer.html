I am an undergraduate student. I will try my best to make sure my essays are as reliable as possible, but if you think I'm wrong about something, there's a high chance that you may be right. I've made a feedback page; please shoot me a message if you think that is the case. I may not have the chance to address it right away, so I will highlight regions that others have flagged so that reader may read with caution.

There will be occasional notes in blue.
asides discuss related things that I had found confusing and/or interesting. They may assume knowledge that I have not listed at the beginning of the essay, but I don't list them there because they are not essential to the content of the essay.
qs are follow-up questions I still have.
todos are topics I need to do to make the essay more comprehensive. When I publish an essay I do so because they are able to stand alone, but there may be discussion that I would want to add in the future to make them more comprehensive.
